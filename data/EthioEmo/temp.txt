EthioEmo dataset was not integrated into this project.

The EthioEmo dataset covers Ethiopian languages (Amharic, Afaan Oromo, Tigrinya,
Somali) which are outside the Bantu language family scope of this work. Attempts
to obtain the dataset were unsuccessful within the project timeline.

This limitation is noted in the written report (documentation/report.tex,
Section 6 -- Conclusion and Future Work).
